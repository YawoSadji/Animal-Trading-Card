Addax

The Addax has physiological adaptations that allow it to conserve water efficiently.

  • Scientific Name: Addax nasomaculatus
  • Average Length: 4 to 5 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 20 to 25 years
  • Habitat: Sahara and Sahel regions of Africa

The Addax is a desert-dwelling antelope with a sandy-white coat and distinctive twisted horns. Critically endangered, they are adapted to survive in extreme heat and scarcity of resources.
